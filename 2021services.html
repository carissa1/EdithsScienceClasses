Classes Offered on Wednesday


Virtual High School Chemistry(10th - 12th)

Prerequisite: Algebra 1
Course description: This course will be held for 2 hours per week for 32 total sessions. In this course, we will focus on matter, density, the periodic table, mixtures, physical and chemical reactions, atoms, acids and bases, Lewis structures, chemical formulas, and much more. We will use a mixture of cooperative learning, thinking maps, lectures, notes, and lab experiments to assist with understanding the topics. As scientific learners, students will analyze the data collected from experiments, answer questions, and write a lab report.

Curriculum: Glencoe High School Chemistry: Matter and Change (2016 edition). ISBN: 978-0076774609


Virtual Middle School Life Science(6th - 8th)

Course description: This course will be held for 1.5 hours per week for 32 weeks. In this course, we will focus on the characteristics of living things, life cycles, plant and animal cell structure, ecology, genetics, classification of organisms, the structure and function of human cells tissues organs, and organ systems, the structure and function of plants, animal structure and function, biomes, and ecosystems. We will use a mixture of cooperative learning, lectures, notes, and lab experiments to assist with understanding the topics.

Curriculum: Life IScience. Glencoe/Mcgraw-Hill; edition 2011, ISBN: 978-0-07-888002-5


Virtual Middle School Physical Science(7th - 8th)

Course description: This course will be held for 1.5 hours per week for 32 weeks. This course introduces the general principles of physics and chemistry. Topics include measurement, matter, the elements of the periodic table, chemical reactions, acids and bases, motion, Newton's laws of motion, momentum, energy, work, power, heat, thermodynamics, electricity, and magnetism.

Curriculum: Physical Iscience, Glencoe/Mcgraw-Hill; edition 2011, ISBN: 978-0-07-888004-9


Classes Offered on Friday


Virtual High School Physics(11th - 12th) - Subject to demand

Prerequisite: Algebra 2 or may be taken concurrently
Course description: This course will be held for 2 hours per week for 32 weeks. In this course, students will study the concepts of interactions of matter and energy, velocity, accelerations, force, energy, momentum, light, sound, waves, charge, magnetism, and electricity. Students will be challenged to apply their knowledge of the laws of physics to solve physics-related critical thinking problems.

Curriculum: Glencoe Physics: Principles and Problems. 9th Edition 2004. ISBN:978-0078458132


Virtual Middle School Earth Science(6th - 7th)

Course description: This course will be held for 1.5 hours per week for 32 total sessions. In this course, we will focus on maps, minerals, rocks, soil, erosion, volcanoes, plate tectonics, the atmosphere, energy resources, the earth-moon system, and much more. We will use a mixture of cooperative learning, thinking maps, lectures, notes, and lab experiments to assist with understanding the topics.

Curriculum: Earth and Space Science, Glencoe Science, 2011 edition. ISBN: 978-0-07-888003-2
