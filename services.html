In-person and online classes will NOT be offered for the 2023-2024 school year


I have loved sharing my passion for science for nearly ten years with my students. However, I have decided not to teach next year since my homeschooled children will be entering their high school journey. Once my family is set with their school plans, I plan to return to teach in the 2024-2025 school year. By then, I will be able to give my time and attention to my students fully.

Thank you all for your support. Teaching all these years has been a rewarding and amazing experience. I have seen exceptional young students grow and leave for college through all these years. Thank you for letting me be a small part of their education.

Sincerely,
